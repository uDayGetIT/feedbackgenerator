Probing Questions for Android

  • What version of Android are you running?
  • When was the last time you updated your device?
  • Have you recently installed any new apps?
  • Is this issue happening with a specific app or across multiple apps?
  • Have you tried restarting your device?
  • Are you connected to Wi-Fi or using mobile data?
  • Is your device's storage nearly full?
  • Have you noticed any unusual battery drain?
  • Are you using any antivirus or security apps?
  • Have you made any recent changes to your device settings?

Probing Questions for iOS

  • What version of iOS are you running?
  • When was the last time you updated your device?
  • Have you recently installed any new apps?
  • Is this issue happening with a specific app or across multiple apps?
  • Have you tried restarting your device?
  • Are you connected to Wi-Fi or using cellular data?
  • Is your device's storage nearly full?
  • Have you noticed any unusual battery drain?
  • Are you signed in to your iCloud account?
